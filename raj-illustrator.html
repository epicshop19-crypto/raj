Who I am

Crafting worlds
one frame at a time.

I'm Raj M., a freelance 2D illustrator based in India with a passion for storytelling through visual art. My work spans character illustration, cinematic posters, and atmospheric scene art.

With 44K+ followers and a growing body of work, I bring narratives to life with warmth, mood, and a distinctly cinematic aesthetic — heavily influenced by Indian light, architecture, and emotion.
